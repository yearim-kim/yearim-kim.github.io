Yearim Kim

I am an integrated M.S./Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at Seoul National University, where I am a member of MIPAL advised by Prof. Nojun Kwak.

My research focuses on eXplainable AI (XAI) and computer vision — understanding what vision models learn and how they make decisions, from attribution methods to mechanistic and concept-level interpretability.

Before SNU, I received my B.S. in Statistics from Korea University.
