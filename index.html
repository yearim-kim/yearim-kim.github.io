Research

I work on eXplainable AI (XAI) for vision models. My goal is to understand what vision models learn and how they make decisions — spanning fine-grained attribution methods, concept-level and mechanistic interpretability, and unified frameworks that connect local, global, and mechanistic views of a model. I believe that opening up the inner workings of models is a prerequisite for safe, transparent, and trustworthy AI.
